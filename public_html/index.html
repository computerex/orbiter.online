Dynamic Bases

In the pre-alpha/proof of concept release, vessel bases synchronize on simulation load. Players can add new bases here, and the bases synchronize to all the orbinauts who have this plug-in active.

Seamless Multiplayer

Once you have registered an account on this site, players can update the orbiter.online.cfg file in the root orbiter directory with their credentials. Doing this and having the orbiter.online plug-in module will log the player into multiplayer on simulation load. This is a pre-alpha release, so keep in mind that multiplayer at this point in time is very rudimentary. The following is supported at this time:

  • Hover, retro and main thruster values are synchronized.
  • The focus vessel is rendered in everyone else's game world.
Currently all vessels are rendered as Deltagliders no matter the class, although this is a very easy limitation to bypass. MJD is not synchronized! So synchronize it approximately, or the positioning will be off. In Orbiter, if two people are on different times, their planetary bodies will be in different positions. So whilst you may be safely landed in your hangar, you may appear to be inside earth on someone else's computer. Luckily, as soon as the MJD is approximately synchronized, the errors become smaller.

Keep in mind, multiplayer is not the main focus of Orbiter Online.
